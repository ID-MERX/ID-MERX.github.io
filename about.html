Good Academic Curriculum

The University's academic curriculum is constantly updated to suite current demands and trends in the corporate world. The course structures have been updated and new courses have been introduced in the quest to produce graduates prepared for professional and academic career opportunities.

Integration of Ethos in the University Curriculum

The University foundation courses include courses in Ethics, The Social Teachings of the Church and Communication Skills. These programs are aimed at preparing professionals who possess critical thinking skills and an appreciation / recognition of the inherent human dignity.

Supporting Programs For Sports

The University has participated in tournaments at local levels with supporting partners and corporate bodies. The University has active football, basketball, volleyball, rugby, netball and chess teams that practice twice a week at the sports grounds.

Holistic Education

We believe that education should be holistic, it should prepare one, not only academically, but also socially, mentally and spiritually. Throughout the time one is at our institution, these values are instilled using both academic and extra curriculum activities.

Location Convenience

The University is conveniently located in a quiet and suitable place for your studies, away from distractions. The University is close to all essential facilities (Hospital, Shops etc.)

Trusted By

The University is trusted by a wide range of corporates & institutions.
